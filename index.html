Multiprocess Add-on Support

Look up your add-on to find out its multiprocess support. You will need your add-on guid. You can find your add-on guid in one of a few places in your add-on:

  • In the <em:id> of install.rdf
  • In the id of the package.json for a JPM add-on
  • on addons.mozilla.org by going to Tools > Manage My Submissions > select the add-on and scroll down to Technical Details
